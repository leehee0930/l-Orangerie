Admission to the Musée de l'Orangerie

Free of charge reservation for:

  • Holders of a non-time stamped admission: Tickets from third-parties vendors, Paris Museum Pass.
  • Beneficiairies of a free admission (on presentation of proof of entitlement):
    those under 18 years old, EU citizens aged between 18 and 25 years, students of art history, people with disabilities
    (and any accompanier), jobseekers, ICOM card.Full list of entitlements at musee-orangerie.fr

The Enfant et compagnie (Child & Co) rate is reserved for paying adults (maximum 2) residents of the European Union, accompanying a child under 18 years of age.
On compulsory presentation of proof of residence in the European Union.

From October 4, 2024 to January 24, 2025, the late opening rate on Fridays is €10 from 6pm.
